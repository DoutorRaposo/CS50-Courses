Fun Facts about Nicolas Cage

Did you know his real name?

It's Nicolas Kim Coppola.

Did you know he's related to other famous people?

You probably got from the last fact that he is a Coppola. He is the nephew of the famous Francis Ford Coppola and cousin of Sofia Coppola. What most people don't know is that he is also Jason Schwartzmann's cousin!

Do you know when he intends to retire?

Never!

Did you know he directed a movie?

He directed Sonny (2002), that stars James Franco.
